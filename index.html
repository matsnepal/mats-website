Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Everything you need to know about MATS and travelling in Nepal.

What does MATS stand for?▾
MATS stands for Maitri Apartment & Travel Services. Our official business name is Maitri Apartment & Travel Services Pvt. Ltd. The name Maitri means "friendship" in Sanskrit — reflecting our commitment to warm, reliable service. We are registered as a Private Limited Company under the laws of Nepal and hold a valid Travel Agency Licence (No. 4593/082/083).
Where is MATS located?▾
We are based in Mitrapark-7, Kathmandu, Nepal. Our location offers easy access to popular tourist destinations and is close to Tribhuvan International Airport. You can find our exact location via this Google Maps link. For appointments, please contact us on WhatsApp first.
Do you book international flights?▾
Yes! MATS provides both domestic and international flight ticket booking. Whether you are flying within Nepal or travelling to Dubai, Delhi, Bangkok, Singapore, London or anywhere else — contact us on WhatsApp (9823356668) with your travel details and we will find you the best available fare.
Do you offer domestic travel packages?▾
We are currently offering limited domestic packages including the Char Ganesh Pilgrimage and Char Narayan Temples tour. We are actively developing a comprehensive range of Nepal travel packages — religious tours, adventure, wildlife, cultural and wellness experiences. Follow @matsnepal on TikTok for launch announcements.
How do I book a flight or bus ticket?▾
It is simple: (1) Fill in the booking form on this page or contact us on WhatsApp (9823356668). (2) Our team confirms availability and fare. (3) You make payment via eSewa, Khalti, bank transfer or cash. (4) We send your e-ticket directly to your WhatsApp or email. The whole process usually takes 1–2 hours.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept eSewa, Khalti, bank transfer and cash. We do not currently accept international credit cards directly — for international clients, please contact us on WhatsApp to arrange payment.
Do you provide airport pickup?▾
Yes, we can arrange airport pickup and drop-off from Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu. Contact us in advance on WhatsApp (9823356668) with your flight details and we will arrange a vehicle for you.
What is the latest travel information for Nepal?▾
For the most up-to-date travel advisories and entry requirements, visit: Nepal Tourism Board — Latest Travel Updates. For visa information: Tourist Visa Information. For TIMS card requirements: TIMS Card Information.
Is MATS a registered company?▾
Yes. Maitri Apartment & Travel Services Pvt. Ltd. is a fully registered Private Limited Company in Nepal. Travel Agency Licence: 4593/082/083 · Company Reg. No.: 358879 · PAN: 621282090 · Small Industry Reg.: 4240/082/083.
Can I cancel or change my booking?▾
Cancellation and change policies depend on the airline or bus operator. Contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible if you need to make changes — we will check what is possible and guide you through the process. Our full cancellation policy is available on request.

Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025 · Maitri Apartment & Travel Services Pvt. Ltd.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a booking request or contact us, we collect your name, phone number, email address, travel details and any other information you voluntarily provide. We do not collect payment information directly — payments are handled separately via agreed methods (eSewa, Khalti, bank transfer or cash).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your flight, bus or tour booking requests
  • To contact you via WhatsApp, phone or email about your booking
  • To send your e-ticket or booking confirmation
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share your details with airlines, bus operators or accommodation providers strictly as required to complete your booking.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. No transmission over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to contact us on WhatsApp for sensitive communications.

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools. Google Maps embedded on this page may set cookies according to Google's own privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@mats.com.np or on WhatsApp at 9823356668.
